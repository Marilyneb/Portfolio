About Me

I am Marilyne Bassoulou, a creative and technically skilled designer with an MSc in Computer Animation and Visual Effects from Bournemouth University.

I have a background that combines computer science and design, and I’m passionate about bridging the gap between creativity and technology. I love designing visually appealing, user-friendly experiences that are both functional and beautiful. By blending coding skills with design expertise, I focus on creating seamless, innovative solutions. With experience in both design tools and coding, I’m always excited to bring new, innovative ideas to life and explore new possibilities in the design world.
